Our Story: Handcrafted with Love in Sandy Hook

A family tradition of excellence since 2009

Dolce is a family-owned and operated gelato shop nestled in the heart of Sandy Hook. What started as a passion project has blossomed into a beloved local destination where tradition meets innovation.

Our commitment to quality means we source the finest ingredients and craft each batch with care. We believe that great gelato brings people together, creating sweet memories that last a lifetime.

Our Journey

15+ years of sweet moments and memories

2009

The Beginning

Dolce opened its doors in Sandy Hook with a dream: to bring authentic Italian gelato to our community. Starting with just 12 family recipes, we quickly became a local favorite.

2014

Expanding Horizons

After 5 years of perfecting our craft, we expanded our flavor menu to over 30 unique varieties, including seasonal specialties and customer favorites like Bacio and Stracciatella.

2019

A Decade of Excellence

Celebrating 10 years in business, we were honored to be named "Best Gelato Shop" by the Sandy Hook community. We also launched our signature Italian ice line.

2024

50+ Unique Flavors

Today, Dolce proudly offers over 50 handcrafted flavors of gelato, Italian ice, and milkshakes. Every recipe still honors our family traditions while embracing creative innovation.

Meet the Owners

The heart and soul behind Dolce

Family Owned

A Family's Passion for Artisanal Excellence

The story of Dolce begins with a family's deep love for authentic Italian desserts and a dream to share that passion with Sandy Hook. Growing up in an Italian household where food was the centerpiece of every gathering, our founders learned the secrets of traditional gelato-making from their grandmother.

In 2009, they took a leap of faith and opened Dolce, transforming family recipes into a thriving business. Every day, you'll find them in the shop—scooping gelato, perfecting new flavors, and chatting with customers who have become like family.

"We don't just serve gelato," they say. "We create moments of joy. Every scoop is made with the same love and care we'd give to our own family—because our customers are family."
